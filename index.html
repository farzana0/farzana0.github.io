About

Multilinear structure, made useful

Many objects we care about in machine learning — feature attributions, cooperative-game values, learned representations, quantum circuits — can be written as multilinear maps. Tensor networks give a compact, tractable handle on them, and that idea runs through my work on explainability and AI safety.
